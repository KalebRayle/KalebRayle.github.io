Basic Step by Step of Building a PC
Step 1: Remove the motherboard from plastic and set on box TIP: Make sure your area is static free
Step 2: Begin installing parts, Start with the CPU TIP: Line the golden triangle on the cpu with the triangle on the socket. DO NOT PUSH IT IN
Step 3: Move on to the RAM and instal the on the slots typically right of the cpu TIP: Push these ones, line them up in every other slot
Step 4: At this point instal either your NVME SSD or the wires for a HDD or SDD TIP: Do not forget your heat sheild for the NVME (Not speaking from experince)
Step 5: Now the fun, Plug in your GPU TIP: Newer GPUs have 8 port cord that must plug in but older ones do not
Step 6: Now install the motherboard into the case and attach all necessary cables TIP: Take the time to cable manage, you'll thank yourself later
